ABOUT US

VMC (Video Messenger Company) was formed in 1989 as a sports promotions company using TV's to communicate messages and advertisements to the sports bar market. The company became a recognized leader as a high quality manufacturer of television based character generators, point of purchase electronic displays (POP) and messaging devices
